Threats to Wildlife

Understanding the challenges that endanger biodiversity

Habitat Loss

Over 60% of India's forest cover has reduced, forcing species to migrate or vanish. Urbanization, mining and agriculture are key culprits.

Climate Change

Shifts temperature, rainfall patterns, breeding cycles and can alter entire ecosystems, pushing species toward extinction.

Poaching & Illegal Trade

India loses hundreds of endangered animals like tigers, elephants, and pangolins every year to wildlife trafficking and black markets.
