Species Categories

Mammals

India is home to Bengal Tigers, Asiatic Lions, Indian Elephants, and Leopards. Over 410 species of mammals are found in various forests and sanctuaries.

Birds

With over 1300 species, India is a haven for birdwatchers. Major species include the Indian Peafowl, Great Indian Bustard, and Hornbills.

Reptiles    &   Amphibians

India supports a wide range of reptiles like King Cobras, Gharials, and turtles. There are over 500 species of reptiles and amphibians combined.

Conservation Status

Endangered

Species like the Bengal Tiger, Asiatic Lion, and Gharial are critically endangered and protected under the Wildlife Protection Act 1972.

Vulnerable

Species such as the Snow Leopard, Indian Pangolin, and Red Panda fall under this category. Conservation projects are ongoing.
